Integrated protection system for pump and compressor pipes
ES System is a system for modifying tubing and tubing couplings, which serves to multiply the service life of the column in the well and corrosion protection, designed to reduce the number of premature failures of the column elements
Technical conditions of application
5 times
Reduction in the volume of purchases of "black" tubing for complicated well stock
+270°C
The heat resistance of stainless steel liners, which significantly exceeds the resistance of any paintwork
5 times
The abrasive resistance of ES-System liners is higher, and the surface roughness comparable to the paintwork and prevents the formation of various deposits on the inner surface (ARPD)
3 times
Higher thread service life due to metallization coating + absence of transportation lubricants facilitates the preparation of pipes for descent
ES-System tubing and couplings protection system
  1. Thin-walled stainless steel pipe
  2. Thin-walled tubing made of carbon steel
  3. Streamer
  4. Tubing coupling
Modified items
  1. TUBING body: installation of the liner
  2. TUBING thread: anti-corrosion coating, dry lubrication
  3. Coupling thread: hardening, anti-corrosion protection
Additional items
  1. Sealer made of fluoropolymers (chemical inertness to all aggressive substances, heat resistance up to +270 C, wear resistance)
  2. Mass – produced streamer
Main characteristic
Assortment
Smooth pipes Ø60- 114mm of any strength group with triangular and trapezoidal threads
