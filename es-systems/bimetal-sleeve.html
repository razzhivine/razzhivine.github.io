Bimetal sleeve for the internal weld protection
CPSС weld protection sleeves are designed to protect the weld seam of the pipeline against corrosion, used on pipelines with internal corrosion protection coating for the transportation of petroleum products, stable condensate, not drained associated petroleum gas, Waste Water with Hydrogen Sulfide Content
Installation instructions
Description
The sleeve is installed inside the pipe in the area of the weld. When installing the sleeve into the pipe, rubber cuffs form a sealed roller from a pre-applied special two-component mastic. The result is an annular weld, completely protected from contact with aggressive environment.
Inert to most corrosive environments
Slight narrowing of the flow area
Light weight
No coating that may be damaged during installation
Brand CPSC.

  • Product range: ⌀ 90 — 530;
  • Larger sleeves can be made to special order;
  • Maximum temperature of the pumped medium: +90 °C;
  • The minimum length of the straight section for the installation of the sleeve is at least 100 mm, the non-paint zone of the pipe ends is 50 mm
Shelf life: 5 years
Service life: 20 years
