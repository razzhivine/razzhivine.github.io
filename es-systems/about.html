About Us

«ES System GMBH» is engaged in the production and sale of devices for protecting the internal part of the weld joint of pipelines (sleeves) with the original design, as well as the production and sale of modified tubing (ES System)

Our company
A modern company with its own production facilities and warehouse complexes, equipped with the latest high-quality equipment and a team of highly qualified specialists
Specialization
Production of devices for protecting the inner part of the welded joint of pipelines (sleeves), as well as the production of modified tubing (ES Sytem)
Production
We have a powerful production complex, which includes: input/output control areas, painting line, machining and welding area, installation work area, pipe transfer system between cleaning, spraying, cladding and hydraulic distribution areas, finished product warehouse
Our team
The basis of our company is a high—class, qualified team of professionals with extensive experience and high competence in the field of corrosion protection of various elements of pipelines and tubing
Why do you choose us

For effective work, the company's specialists are constantly in search of modern solutions, approaching the issue of corrosion protection taking in light of the latest trends.

All work is carried out on modern high-performance equipment: semi-automatic welding in a shielding gas environment, surface preparation in a manned shot blasting chamber, painting parts on a specialized line.
