Types of internal protection sleeves of the weld

The sleeve is installed inside the pipe in the area of the weld. When installing the sleeve into the pipe, rubber cuffs form a sealed roller from a pre-applied special two-component mastic. The result is an annular weld, completely protected from contact with aggressive environment.

Used in systems transporting oil and gas mixtures, non-drained petroleum gas and oil products, drinking, reservoir and other technical water, including hydrogen sulfide, carbon dioxide and other corrosion-active components in oil fields

Mastic Sleeve for the internal Weld Protection

Bimetal sleeve for the internal weld protection
CPSС sleeve is the latest development in the protection of the inner part of welds and is made of stainless steel
